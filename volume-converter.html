Understanding Volume and Its Units

Volume is a measure of the three-dimensional space occupied by a substance or enclosed by a boundary. It is a fundamental property in physics, chemistry, and engineering, essential for quantifying fluid capacities, object sizes, and material amounts. The standard international (SI) unit for volume is the cubic meter (mÂ³).
