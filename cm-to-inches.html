How to Convert cm to Inches

To convert centimeters to inches, divide the length in centimeters by 2.54 since 1 inch = 2.54 cm.

Formula: inches = centimeters ÷ 2.54

Example: 10 cm ÷ 2.54 = 3.937 inches
